Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

1. Introduction

AppleGPT ("we," "us," or "our") operates the AppleGPT mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using AppleGPT, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Apple Sign-In Information

When you sign in with Apple, we collect:

  • Unique Apple User ID (required for accountability)
  • Email address (if you choose to share it)
  • Full name (if you choose to share it)

2.2 Chat Data

We store:

  • Chat sessions and message history (stored locally on your device)
  • For Premium users: chat history synced to cloud
  • Session timestamps and metadata

2.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage statistics
  • Firebase Analytics data (crashes, performance metrics)

2.4 Subscription & Payment Data

If you subscribe to Premium:

  • Subscription status and expiration date
  • Purchase history (processed by Apple, not stored by us)

3. Why We Collect This Data

3.1 Legal Accountability

Important: We require authentication because AI conversations must be linked to verified identities. This protects our service and users from:

  • Misuse of AI capabilities
  • Harmful or illegal content generation
  • Abuse and spam
  • Fraud and impersonation

3.2 Service Functionality

  • Sync your chats across devices (Premium)
  • Maintain your subscription status
  • Provide ad-free experience for Premium users

4. On-Device Processing

Your AI conversations are processed entirely on your device using Apple's Foundation Models. Your prompts and responses are never sent to external AI servers. This ensures maximum privacy for your conversations.

5. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored as follows:

  • Chat history: Locally on your device (default)
  • Premium cloud sync: Google Firebase servers (United States)
  • User accounts: Google Firebase Firestore
  • Retention: Until you delete your account

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Google Firebase (cloud hosting for Premium sync)
  • Google AdMob (advertising for free users)
  • Apple App Store (payment processing)
  • When required by law or court order

7. Your Rights

7.1 Access Your Data

Contact us to request a copy of your data.

7.2 Delete Your Account

Go to: Profile ‚Üí Delete Account

This permanently deletes:

  • Your user profile
  • All cloud-synced chat history
  • Subscription data
  • Cannot be undone

7.3 Delete Local Chats

Use the Cloud Options menu to delete all local chat history at any time.

8. Security

We implement reasonable security measures:

  • Secure data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Authentication-required API access
  • On-device AI processing (no external servers)
  • Data stored on Google Firebase infrastructure
